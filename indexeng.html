The Hajós-Baja Wine Region comprises 15 settlements in the south-western part of the Great Plain region. Due to the excellent soil and the flat area, a large amount of rain falls suddenly from the area, so the more water-demanding, high-quality white wine grape varieties can be grown. (Eg Chardonnay, Rhine Riesling, Cserszeg Spicy.)
